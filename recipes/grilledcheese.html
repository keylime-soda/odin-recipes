Grilled Cheese

I mean, it's grilled cheese. Pretty simple

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A reasonable amount of cheese
  • About a tblsp butter

The work:

Butter one side of both slices of bread.

Toast one slice over medium to low heat.

PILE ON THE CHEESE

Add the other slice of bread, butter side up

FLIP

Toast the other side until golden brown and the cheeze is all melty goodness!

EAT
