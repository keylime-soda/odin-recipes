Grilled Cheese

I mean, it's grilled cheese. Pretty simple

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A reasonable amount of cheese
  • About a tblsp butter

The work:

  1. Butter one side of both slices of bread.
  2. Toast one slice over medium to low heat.
  3. PILE ON THE CHEESE
  4. Add the other slice of bread, butter side up
  5. FLIP
  6. Toast the other side until golden brown and the cheeze is all melty goodness!
  7. EAT
